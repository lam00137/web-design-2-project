Beer Wings

Beer wings are a tasty appetizer that is perfect for game day or any gathering with friends. The crispy chicken wings are coated in a flavorful beer batter that will leave your taste buds wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup beer

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together flour, salt, garlic powder, paprika, and black pepper.
  3. Add beer to the dry ingredients and whisk until a smooth batter forms.
  4. Dip each chicken wing into the batter, making sure it's fully coated, then place it on the baking sheet.
  5. Bake the chicken wings for 45-50 minutes, or until crispy and golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven and serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
